# Background Music

Place your cyberpunk background music file here:

- **cyberpunk-bg.mp3** (preferred format)
- **cyberpunk-bg.ogg** (fallback format)

The music will automatically loop when the user clicks the music toggle button in the header.

## Recommended Music Sources:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Free Music Archive
- Incompetech
- Pixabay Music

Make sure you have the rights to use the music on your portfolio!
